FOCUS TWIN — Product Requirements Document
============================================

1. Executive Summary
--------------------
Focus Twin is a mobile-first Progressive Web App (PWA) that gives every user a personal AI productivity companion — their "Evo Twin." The twin lives entirely on-device, learns the user's unique focus patterns, and provides personalized coaching to help them build sustainable deep work habits. No data ever leaves the user's phone. Revenue comes from a freemium model (₹149/month premium).


2. Problem Statement
--------------------
The productivity tool market is split into two broken camps:

A) Dumb Timers (Forest, Pomodoro apps)
   - Set a timer, stare at it, done
   - Zero intelligence — doesn't learn you, doesn't adapt
   - No insights — "you focused for 25 minutes" tells you nothing useful
   - Result: novelty wears off in 1-2 weeks

B) Surveillance Dashboards (RescueTime, Rize, ActivTrak)
   - Track every app, every website, every second
   - Upload everything to cloud servers
   - Feel invasive — users describe them as "creepy"
   - Show data without actionable advice — "you spent 4 hours on YouTube" — so what?
   - Privacy risk: your entire digital behavior on someone's server

The Gap: Nobody offers an intelligent, private, emotionally engaging focus companion that learns YOUR patterns and tells you what to actually DO differently.


3. Target Users
---------------
Primary: Students (18-25) preparing for exams, placements, competitive coding
   - Pain: Constant distraction, guilt cycle, no structured focus system
   - Willingness to pay: Already spending on Forest, productivity courses
   - Scale: 50M+ students in India alone actively seeking productivity tools

Secondary: Remote workers / freelancers (22-35)
   - Pain: No office structure, work-from-home distractions, burnout
   - Willingness to pay: Already using Notion, Todoist, calendar tools
   - Scale: 300M+ remote workers globally post-COVID


4. Core Value Proposition
-------------------------
"Your AI twin that learns how YOU focus — privately, on your phone, getting smarter every day."

Three pillars:
1. INTELLIGENT — Not a dumb timer. An AI agent learns your patterns, coaches you in natural language.
2. PRIVATE — Data stays on-device. AI coaching uses only computed stats, never raw data.
3. ALIVE — An animated AI companion that speaks, adapts, and grows with you.


5. Functional Requirements & User Flows
-----------------------------------------

5.1 Onboarding (< 30 seconds)
   - App opens → Animated splash → "Meet your Focus Twin"
   - Quick setup: Name your twin (optional), pick your goal (study/work/create)
   - No signup required — app works immediately with local storage
   - Optional: Email/Google login for cross-device sync (encrypted, E2E)

5.2 Home Dashboard
   - Twin avatar center screen with current mood (idle/focused/celebrating/tired)
   - AI-GENERATED GREETING: Twin speaks a personalized message based on your patterns,
     streak, time of day, and recent trend (powered by LLM agent — see arch.txt)
     Example: "Afternoon! Your 2pm slot is usually golden — ready to crush it?"
   - Today's challenge card (AI-generated, adapts to your history)
   - Quick stats: Today's focus time, current streak, weekly trend
   - "Start Focus" button — primary CTA

5.3 Focus Session
   - Tap "Start Focus" → Full-screen focus mode
   - Twin enters focus animation (meditating/working pose)
   - Optional: Ambient sounds (rain, lo-fi, white noise, cafe)
   - Minimal UI: Timer + twin + "End Session" button
   - No distracting stats during session — just you and your twin
   - Session ends naturally or when user taps "End"

5.4 Post-Session Feedback (AI Agent Powered)
   - AI-GENERATED celebration message based on session stats + user history:
     "47 minutes on a Wednesday! That's 10 more than your usual mid-week. Keep it up! 🔥"
   - Quick reflection prompt (optional): "How was that session?" (Great / Okay / Tough)
   - AI-GENERATED insight card: personalized coaching based on patterns:
     "You focus best 2-5pm. Tomorrow's a Thursday — historically your strongest day.
      Let's aim for 55 minutes."
   - Twin earns XP → visual growth animation
   - All AI responses generated from computed stats only (never raw data)
   - Falls back to template responses if no LLM available

5.5 Insights & Analytics (Key Differentiator)
   - Daily: Focus time, session count, best streak, mood correlation
   - Weekly: Pattern chart — which days/hours are your peak focus times
   - Monthly: Trend analysis — "Your average focus streak grew from 25 to 42 minutes"
   - AI Insights (powered by Twin Agent):
     * "You lose focus after 3+ meetings — try a 10-minute walk between meetings"
     * "Your Monday mornings are 60% more productive than Friday afternoons"
     * "When you rate sessions as 'Tough', you usually recover by taking a 20-minute break"
   - All data processed and stored locally. Never leaves device.

5.5b Chat Mode (Premium — Talk to Your Twin)
   - User can ask their twin anything about their focus patterns:
     User: "Why can't I focus after 5pm?"
     Twin: "Looking at your data — focus drops 60% after 5pm. But on days when you
            take a break between 4-5pm, your evening sessions are 40% better.
            Try a walk at 4:30?"
   - Twin has memory of past conversations (stored locally in IndexedDB)
   - Twin personality adapts to your coaching preferences (motivational/analytical/gentle)
   - LLM receives only computed stats + chat history, never raw session data

5.6 Twin Evolution System
   - Twin starts as basic avatar with 3 moods (idle, focus, celebrate)
   - Level up through consistent focus sessions:
     * Level 1 (Day 1): Basic twin, 3 moods, template responses
     * Level 5 (Week 1): New mood animations, particle effects, AI greetings unlock
     * Level 10 (Week 2): Twin speaks AI insights, personality traits emerge
     * Level 15 (Week 3): Predictive coaching unlocks ("tomorrow looks like a good focus day")
     * Level 20 (Month 1): Full personality, chat mode unlocks, advanced animations
   - Streak bonuses: 7-day streak → special twin animation, 30-day → rare visual upgrade
   - Twin personality evolves: starts generic, becomes uniquely yours over time
   - This creates switching cost: "My twin KNOWS me after 3 months — I can't start over"

5.7 Daily Micro-Challenges
   - One personalized challenge per day based on your history:
     * "Beat yesterday's longest streak by 5 minutes"
     * "Do 3 focus sessions today (your average is 2)"
     * "Try focusing in your dead zone (Friday 4pm) — break the pattern"
   - Completing challenges = Twin XP + streak maintenance
   - Missing a day = Streak pauses (not resets — we're kind, not punishing)


6. Monetization
---------------
Free Tier:
   - Focus timer with twin avatar (3 moods)
   - Basic daily challenge
   - Simple stats (today + this week)
   - 2 ambient sound options

Premium (₹149/month or ₹999/year):
   - Full twin evolution (all moods, animations, personality)
   - Advanced AI insights (pattern detection, predictions, coaching)
   - Weekly/monthly deep reports
   - All ambient sounds + ability to add custom sounds
   - Custom focus session types (deep work, light work, creative, study)
   - Widget for home screen showing today's challenge


7. Success Metrics (MVP KPIs)
-----------------------------
   - Time to first focus session: < 60 seconds from app launch
   - Day 7 retention: > 40% (industry avg for productivity apps is ~20%)
   - Day 30 retention: > 20%
   - Daily active sessions: > 1.5 per active user
   - Free to premium conversion: > 5% within 30 days
   - Average focus streak length growth: > 15% month-over-month


8. What's NOT in MVP (Explicitly Excluded)
------------------------------------------
   - Social features / leaderboards (maybe v2)
   - Web browser extension for website blocking (maybe v2)
   - Wearable integration / Apple Watch (v3)
   - Team/enterprise features (v3)
   - Any cloud sync (v1 is 100% on-device)
   - Any blockchain, tokens, ZK proofs, or Web3 features (permanently removed)


9. Competitive Positioning
--------------------------
   Forest:       Dumb timer. Cute but no intelligence. We add AI + personality.
   RescueTime:   Smart but creepy. Cloud-based surveillance. We add privacy + companion.
   Centered:     AI nudges but cloud-based. No emotional connection. We add privacy + twin.
   ActivityWatch: Private but no AI, no UX, no coaching. We add intelligence + design.
   Rize:         AI tracking but cloud-based, dashboard-only. We add privacy + companion.

   Focus Twin = Forest's simplicity + RescueTime's intelligence + Tamagotchi's emotional hook + ActivityWatch's privacy
